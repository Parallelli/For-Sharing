Where to hike
  1. Grose Mt https://www.grousemountain.com/grousegrind
    1. it's the peak of Vancouver, from where you can get a bird view of Vancouver
    2. close to the metro area and you can take a bus there, although I recommend driving by yourself to save time
  2. Squamish mt. https://www.exploresquamish.com/
    1. There's 2 hiking trails, one is called "cliff", I forgot the other one (I hiked the second one, which is easier)
    2. it's a very cost effective hike because there are 3 amazing scenic viewpoints.
    3. it's a ~4 hour hike up and then you can choose to take gondola up and down
    4. there's a small bridal veil fall nearby 
  3. Joffre lake https://www.vancouvertrails.com/trails/joffre-lakes/
    1. must-hike. There's 3 glacier lakes along the hike.
    2. The level of difficulty is medium, well, easy to medium.
    3. It's near Whistler, which is one of the most famous tourist places in Vancouver.
  4. (If you have more energy) Garibodi lake. https://www.vancouvertrails.com/trails/garibaldi-lake/
    1. It's a hard one, will take likely 5+ hours up and 2hours down
    2. There's a glacier lake on the peak, as well as a fantastic viewpoint midway
  5. (If you wanna try something easy but still with amazing view) Cheakamus Lake https://www.vancouvertrails.com/trails/cheakamus-lake/
    1. it's just easy and beautiful.
